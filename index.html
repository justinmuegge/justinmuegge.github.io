Justin Muegge (b. 1995, Novato, CA) is a painter based in the San Francisco Bay Area. His work explores fragmented views of Chinese Scholar's Gardens — architectural thresholds, rock formations, and cultivated landscapes broken apart and reassembled through collage. He received his MFA from Hunter College in 2025.
